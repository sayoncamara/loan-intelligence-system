CREDIT SCORE LENDING POLICY

Minimum credit score requirements:
- Grade A loans: FICO score 750 and above
- Grade B loans: FICO score 700-749
- Grade C loans: FICO score 660-699
- Grade D loans: FICO score 630-659
- Grade E loans: FICO score 600-629
- Grade F/G loans: FICO score below 600 (high risk, limited approval)

Applicants with FICO scores below 580 are considered high risk and will 
typically be denied unless compensating factors are present such as 
low debt-to-income ratio or significant assets.
