LOAN PURPOSE AND TERM POLICY

Loan term risk assessment:
- 36-month loans: Standard risk, preferred term
- 60-month loans: Higher risk due to extended exposure

High risk loan purposes:
- Small business loans carry elevated default risk
- Debt consolidation loans require DTI below 40%

Employment requirements:
- Minimum 1 year employment history preferred
- Less than 1 year employment requires compensating factors
- Unknown employment history increases risk assessment
